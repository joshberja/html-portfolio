The Best Movies According to Joshua Berja

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Heat

This is my favorite action-drama film from 1995. A classic for sure.

Napoleon Dynamite

One of my favorite comedy movies. This 2004 film can give some good laughs.

Terminator 2

Classic 1990s film filled with many quotes. I used to watch this with my grandpa with his VHS tape and VHS player.
